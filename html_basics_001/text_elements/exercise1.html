Pet Sitting Instruction

Dear Maria, thank you very much for volunteering to pet-sit for us. In this document

Grizzly

Grizzly is our adorable yellow lab happy
Please give grizzly 21/2 cups of his kibble every day. Most importantly, make sure
that no matter how much he begs or whines, never feed him extra food.

Princess Popcorn

nightmare right now. We have to do something to do something about it. Please follow these cleaning
instructions:

  1. Remove Princess Popcorn
  2. Clean the inside glass
  3. Siphon the water
  4. Clean the fliter
  5. Return Princess Popcorn to the tank
